PKD code

You must choose a major code describing your core business. You can also choose several additional codes

When registering your business, you will need to choose at least one code of business activity according to Polska Klasyfikacja Działalności, or PKD.

The codes are used for statistical purposes, but they also have some practical significance. They are associated with specific forms of taxation, VAT requirement, or the requirement to use a fiscal register.

You should choose one main code for your business activity, describing your main business area that will generate the majority of your revenues.

Additionally, you can choose as many additional codes as you want. These will correspond to the activities that you perform, but are not your major activity.

 

If our main business is photography, your main code will be PKD 74.20.Z Działalność fotograficzna. Additionally, you might want to choose such codes as "PKD 18.13.Z - Działalność usługowa związana z przygotowywaniem do druku" or "PKD 59.11.Z Działalność związana z produkcją filmów, nagrań wideo i programów telewizyjnych".

Do not register too many codes „just in case”. If you want to expand your business, you can always add a new code or codes to your registration.

If you change the scope or nature of your business, you are required to update the codes within 7 days of such change. .

Certain codes are reserved for individual farmers or for commercial law companies, and you cannot choose them. Always check the PKD website.

Where to find the codes

Use the search function available at the PKD website.

You will find there a five-tier register – first you choose one general area, and then – more detailed sections, groups and classes.

If you cannot find the appropriate code for your business, use a contact form (in Polish ) to write to Ośrodek Klasyfikacji i Nomenklatur Urzędu Statystycznego w Łodzi. If you describe the nature of your business, an officer will help you select the appropriate code. The form is available in Polish only, so you might need help from a Polish-speaking friend.

How to change a code/add a new code

At any time, you can register any of the following changes:

  • a change of your main business – change your main code
  • extend the scope of your business – add new PKD codes for new activities
  • update the scope of your business – delete the PKD codes for the activities that you no longer perform

If your activity is a regulated activity or requires a licence or a permit, you are required to notify any change of the PKD codes to the authority that issued the relevant permit or licence. You should do this within 7 days of the date of the change.

 

 

  1. Consult the PKD website
  2. Choose one major code for your business,
  3. Optionally, choose one or more additional codes.
  4. Enter the code(s) in section 5.1 of your registration application.
