How to fill in the registration form

The section below shows you how to fill in the registration form on paper, but it will be helpful also if you choose an electronic form. The order of the fields and the information required are the same, so just have this guide at hand when filling in your application online.

Fill in section 1 of the registration form - purpose of the registration

Fill in section 2 of the registration form - your personal data and your foreigner status

If you are a foreigner, you can use such identity documents as a passport, a temporary ID card, a temporary proof of identity, an identity document issued in your country of origin, a permanent residence card, a temporary residence card.

In section 2.2, give the details of your identity document: its number, the name of the body that issued it, the date of issue.

You can read more in the chapter about Foreigner status.

Fill in sections 3 and 4 of the registration form - your address and contact details

Fill in section 5 of the registration form - name of your business.

How to choose a name for your business - read chapter Name for your business.

How to choose PKD code(s) for your business - read chapter on PKD codes.

 Fill in section 6 of the registration form - your correspondence address

Fill in section 7 of the registration form - the place where you will conduct your business

Which address to choose - read more in chapter on Business address.

 Fill in section 8 of the registration form - additional place of business

What is an additional place of business? Read the chapter Business address.

 Fill in section 9 of the registration form - information about your social security insurance

In section 9, you indicate whether

  • you are subject to social security insurance in Poland - check the first box
  • you have social security insurance in another country - check the third box

Then, depending on your choice, you will need to complete the other sections.

Read more about social security.

 Fill in section 10 of the registration form

By checking this box you confirm that:

  • you are not subject to prohibition to conduct business
  • you have a relevant legal title to the property whose address is given in section 9. What legal title is required? You will find the information in chapter Who can be a sole trader.

 Sections 11, 12 and 13 of the registration form - leave them empty

These sections relate to suspending, resuming or ceasing your business. You can skip them now - you might need to use them in the future.

 Fill in sections 14 and 15 of the registration form - your tax office and the taxation form

Give the name of the tax office - this is the tax office proper for your residence address in Poland.

Choose the taxation form by checking the relevant box. Read more about taxation forms.

 Fill in sections 16, 17 18 and 19 of the registration form - information about your accounting

Check the box in section 16, if you plan to keep full accounting next year. Read more in chapter on Accounting.

In section 17, indicate who will keep your accounting - you or a hired accountant. Read more n chapter on Accounting.

In section 18, the box "YES" is for a protected employment establishment employing persons with disabilities. Choose "NO" - you can run a protected employment establishment only if you have been operating for at least 12 months.

In section 19, give the address of the place where your accounting documentation will be kept. Depending on your choice in section 17, it will be either your address or your accountant's address.

 

 Fill in section 20 of the registration form - information about the tax and social security numbers that you have in other countries

You will need to fill in this section, if you checked the relevant box for "social security insurance in another country" in section 9.

 Fill in section 21 of the registration form - information about your bank account

Enter your business bank account number, if you have a business bank account.

If not, give your private bank account number. Read more about a bank account.

 Fill in sections 23, 24 and 25 of the registration form

In section 23, indicate if you have any assets that you own jointly with your spouse.

In section 24, indicate the form of your business. In the most cases, it will be your individual business, so you need to check the first box.

In section 25, you should state whether you are a partner in any civil-law partnership.

 Fill in section 26 of the registration form - power of attorney

You will need to fill in section 26 only when you appoint an attorney to act on your behalf.

 Fill in section 27 of the registration form - attachments

In section 27, indicate the attachments that you are filing together with your registration application. If you are filing any, write their number in the relevant box.

 Fill in section 28 of the registration form - GDPR information

This section contains information on the processing of your personal data in CEIDG.

You should sign and date the application.

This is the end of your registration application.

If your are completing it online, click "ZŁÓŻ" (Submit).

If you are filling it in on paper, print it. Do not sign it yet. Then send it or file it in person. See the section on how to file your application for details.
