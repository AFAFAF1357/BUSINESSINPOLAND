Sole tradership - pros and cons

Please see below the summary of the advantages and disadvantages of a sole tradership

Advantages Disadvantages
Simple to establish

A sole trader is liable with their personal assets for obligations resulting from running business
No costs of registering business There are no shares, so you cannot sell your business
Best suited for natural persons  

Can be registered online, without complex formalities

 

There is no minimum capital requirement

 

Several forms of taxation are available

 

Simplified accounting

 

You can suspend or de-register your business at any time
