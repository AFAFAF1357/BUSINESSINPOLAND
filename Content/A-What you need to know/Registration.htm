You do not need to register a small-size business activity generating insignificant revenue

When registration is not required (unregistered business activity)

If you plan a small-scale activity and expect to generate an insignificant income only, you can start your business without any formalities – you do not need to register your business.

You can run an unregistered business activity, if you meet all of the following conditions:

  1. Your revenue in a single month does not exceed 50% of the minimum remuneration (in 2023, the minimum salary is PLN 3490 in the first half of the year – until 30 June, and PLN 3600 in the second half of the year, from 1 July). It means that you cannot earn more than PLN 1745 a month in the first half of the year, and more than PLN 1800 a month in the second half) . Current information on the average salary and the minimum salary is published by the Social Security Office.
  2. You are a natural person
  3. You do not operate a civil-law partnership
  4. You do not run a regulated activity, i.e., an activity that requires a permit or a licence
  5. You have not been conducting any business activity for the last 60 months.

 

You do not need to register a business, if you plan to operate as a farmer and engage in

  • offering accommodation to holidaymakers (agroturystyka)
  • wine production
  • retail trade in agricultural products.

 

 

You can run an unregistered business activity while working under an employment contract. This is a good and safe way to test your business in a safe environment, without quitting your permanent job.

Advantages:

  • Limited costs and formalities . You do not need to register your business with CEIDG, tax office or the Central Statistical Office – you do not need a NIP or REGON number.
  • You do not pay social and health insurance contributions. If you operate under a mandate agreement (umowa zlecenie), your client will be responsible for paying any social insurance contributions.
  • You do not need to pay monthly tax advances. Your yearly income will be included in your annual tax declaration PIT-36.
  • You do not need to keep full accounting records. A simplified sales register will be sufficient.
  • As a result, an unregistered business activity is exempt from VAT, except for some types of activity that require a VAT status regardless of revenues.

What are your duties:

  • Record every product or service sold
  • Issue invoices or bills when a client requests them
  • Respect general consumer rights – right to withdraw from an agreement, to lodge a complaint, etc.

 

You need to monitor your revenues. Once the monthly limit is exceeded, you need to register your business activity within 7 days.
